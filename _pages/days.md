# BiGre Days: December 7th, 2026 @ IMAG

**Save the date !**

Join us for this second edition of the BiGre Day, a one-day conference bringing together the vibrant community of computational biology, biostatistics, bioinformatics, and biophysics in Grenoble.

We aim to provide a platform for young local researchers to share their work and foster collaboration.

More information (abstract submission, registration, schedule) to be announced soon.

The event will take place in the [IMAG](https://batiment.imag.fr/public/plan/) building's main amphitheater.

## Important dates

- **Conference**: December 7th 2026
- **Call for abstract and registration** : Autumn 2026, TBA
